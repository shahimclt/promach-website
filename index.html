What we do

Our Products

Vertical Shaft Impactor

Vertical Shaft Impactor (VSI) crushers are designed to be used in secondary, tertiary or quaternary stage crushing.

Vibrating Screens

Vibrating Screens are the most important screening machines primarily utilised in the mineral processing industry.

Grizzly Feeders

Grizzly Feeders are engineered to combine the functions of feeding and scalping into one unit, reducing the cost of having two separate units.

Pan feeders

Pan feeders are designed to feed an intermediate scalper or a primary crusher directly.

Bucket Wheel Classifier

Bucket Wheel Classifier is an equipment which gives better quality product than any other washing equipment.

Conveyor accessories

Promach provides the most complete and versatile range of conveyor parts and accessories on the market to maximize the efficiency and safety of the conveyor operations.
