    Establishing Industry Standards by Optimized Solutions

    The mining and aggregates industry is one of the major industry sectors in India, It is one of the largest contributors to economic activities. Construction industry employs roughly 35million people and it is the significant driver for FDI inflows. 10% of the INDIA’s GDP share is from the associated construction industry. Without any doubt it is one of the most dynamic and highly fragmented industries in India. It's a Herculean task, to establish benchmarks in such industries, But with combined efforts and dedication Promach has achieved benchmarks and created new industry standards.

    Promach uses unique and optimized engineering solutions to each project. Promach is equipped with a highly experienced and creative engineering team, who are dedicated to study in detail about each project and offer customised technology solutions as per the requirement of the clients. Promach uses cutting edge technologies in the most efficient format to overcome challenges.

    The Vertical Shaft Impactor can be seen as a typical example. It uses Rock on Rock technology for better crushing. This yields more quality and efficient output than traditional machines, The result is premium quality sand. Quality Carbide tips on technology trends helps Promach to gain advantage over machinery maintenance.

    Collaboration of forign and domestic technologies gives competitive advantage and helps to deliver premium quality services to clients. Typical example is Bucket wheel Classifier, The machinery is custom engineered with German and Italian Technology, which give below 10% slurry wastage. Decreasing wastage is always a challenge in the aggregate industry.

    Promach establishes new industry benchmarks by using advanced technological use and optimizing technical processes. Promachs custom approach in every project assures unique solutions to every client. Promach destined to be the perfect catalyst for aggregate industry operations.
